Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2024

The Oracle Feed ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our automation tool handles information when you use our services to publish content to YouTube Shorts.

1. Information We Collect

Our application only accesses the specific data required to perform video uploads. This includes:

  • OAuth Tokens: We securely store authorization tokens provided by YouTube to publish content on your behalf.
  • Media Content: We temporarily process the video files and captions you wish to upload.

2. How We Use Data

Data is used exclusively for the purpose of content automation. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not use your content for training external models without your explicit permission.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app interacts with the YouTube and TikTok APIs. Your use of these platforms through our service is subject to the their respective Privacy Policies. We are not responsible for how these platforms handle your data once it has been uploaded.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your tokens and content during the upload process. On GitHub Actions, all sensitive keys are stored in encrypted repository secrets.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@botzies.playground.

🔒 Privacy Policy

FileNotClosed — TikTok Content App

This Privacy Policy explains how the FileNotClosed app collects, uses, and protects your information when you connect your TikTok account.

Owner MR ZAR
App Name FileNotClosed
Platform TikTok API
Last Updated April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize this application through TikTok's OAuth flow, we may collect:

  • Your TikTok User ID and basic profile information (display name, avatar) — via the user.info.basic scope.
  • An access token and refresh token to enable content publishing on your behalf — via the video.publish scope.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Authenticating your TikTok account to allow automated video publishing.
  • Uploading AI-generated short-form videos to your TikTok account according to your configured schedule.
  • Refreshing your access token to maintain a continuous, authorized connection to TikTok.

⚠️ We do not sell, share, rent, or trade your personal information with any third parties, advertisers, or data brokers.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your access tokens and account identifiers are stored securely as encrypted environment variables within GitHub Actions Secrets. We do not store your information in any external databases. Access to these credentials is strictly limited to the automated pipeline.

4. TikTok Permissions (Scopes)

This app requests only the minimum required scopes:

  • user.info.basic — To verify your account identity.
  • video.publish — To upload and publish videos to your TikTok account on your behalf.

5. Your Rights & Data Deletion

You can revoke this app's access to your TikTok account at any time by visiting TikTok → Settings → Security → Authorized Apps and removing "FileNotClosed". Upon revocation, all stored tokens become invalid and are rendered useless.

To request deletion of any personal data we hold, contact us at the email below.

6. Third-Party Services

This app integrates with the following third-party APIs to generate video content:

  • TikTok API — Content publishing.
  • ElevenLabs API — AI voice generation.
  • Pexels API — Stock footage sourcing.
  • Cohere / Groq / OpenAI APIs — AI story and caption generation.

Each of these services is governed by its own privacy policy. We do not share your TikTok credentials with any of these services.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the application after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
